Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Section 8, leasing, and our veteran programs.

Do you accept Section 8 Vouchers?
Yes! 100% of our properties are Section 8 and HUD-VASH eligible. We handle the paperwork with the housing authority to make the process smooth for you.
Is there an application fee?
No. We believe in removing barriers to housing. Our initial application process is free for veterans and families with vouchers.
How fast is the move-in process?
Once your voucher is approved and the inspection is passed (which we guarantee), you can typically move in within 3-5 days.
What if I don't qualify for Section 8?
We offer both voucher-accepted and market rate properties. Our renovations keep prices affordable with fair market rates starting at $800/month.
Do you work with veterans specifically?
Absolutely. As a veteran-owned company, we prioritize veteran families and partners with VA programs. We'll help you navigate VA benefits and voucher processes.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve East Texas including Tyler, Longview, Jefferson, and surrounding areas. We can help with housing authority applications regardless of location.
Are pet deposits required?
Many of our properties are pet-friendly. We offer flexible pet policies with reasonable deposits. Some Section 8 properties have additional pet program requirements.
