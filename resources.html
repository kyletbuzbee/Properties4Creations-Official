Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers

The primary source of federal housing assistance for families and individuals.

1

Apply for Section 8

Contact your local Public Housing Authority (PHA). Priority is often given to homeless individuals.

2

Get Pre-Approved

Receive your voucher stating the maximum amount of rent assistance available.

3

Find Housing

Search for properties that accept Section 8. We specialize in working with these vouchers.

Section 8 Eligibility

  • ? Household income at or below 50% of area median
  • ? U.S. citizenship or eligible immigrant status
  • ? Positive landlord history references

Priority Groups

Homeless individuals, Veterans, Elderly households (62+), Disabled individuals.
