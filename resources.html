Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers

The primary source of federal housing assistance helps families and individuals with incomes at or below 30-80% of the area median income.

1

Apply for Section 8

Contact your local Public Housing Authority (PHA) to apply. Priority is given to homeless individuals and families.

2

Get Pre-Approved

Once approved, you'll receive a voucher stating the maximum amount of rent assistance you can receive.

3

Find Housing

Search for properties that accept Section 8. Owners can get fair market value offers from P4C.

4

Move Into Your Home

Once your voucher is approved by the owner, you pay the PHA portion and an affordable portion of the rent.
