From Vision to Reality: 15+ Years of Impact

Founded in 2012 by veteran entrepreneur Richard Lonkert, Properties 4 Creations began as a simple idea: what if we could transform distressed properties into welcoming homes for our fellow veterans and families? Over the years, our mission has grown into a movement‚Äîrestoring hope, dignity, and stability for those who have served and for families in need.
